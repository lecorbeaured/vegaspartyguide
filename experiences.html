Exotic Car Racing Experience

Drive a Lamborghini, Ferrari, or Porsche on the Las Vegas Motor Speedway with a professional instructor. One of the most thrilling activities available in Vegas — perfect for bachelor parties and adrenaline seekers.

Escape Rooms

Vegas has some of the most elaborate escape rooms in the country. Ideal for group bonding — work together to solve puzzles in immersive themed environments. A fun afternoon or early evening activity before hitting the clubs.

From ~$35/person Multiple Strip locations

Top options: Escapology Las Vegas, The Escape Game, Mind Bender Escape

🎡

High Roller Observation Wheel

At 550 feet, the High Roller is the world's tallest observation wheel. Book a "Happy Half Hour" cabin with an open bar and views over the entire Strip — a fun, unique group experience at a very reasonable price.

From ~$45/person LINQ Promenade

Book directly at caesars.com/linq or on Viator for group rates

🚣

Gondola Rides at The Venetian

Romantic and unique — gondola rides through the canals of The Venetian, both indoor and outdoor. A memorable, Instagram-worthy experience that's surprisingly affordable and perfect for couple moments within a group trip.

From ~$29/person The Venetian Resort

Book directly at venetian.com/gondola or walk up during the day

🎳

Brooklyn Bowl Las Vegas

Bowling, live music, and incredible food all under one roof. An iconic venue that's perfect for groups who want a fun, active experience without the intensity of a nightclub. Great for mixed groups or a low-key night out.

From ~$25/lane/hour LINQ Promenade

Book lanes in advance at brooklynbowl.com for peak weekend times

🌋

Red Rock Canyon Day Trip

Just 30 minutes from the Strip, Red Rock Canyon is a stunning natural escape. Guided hiking tours, scenic drives, and breathtaking red sandstone landscapes. A perfect daytime activity to balance the nightlife with something adventurous.

From ~$49/person 30 min from Strip
