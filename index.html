Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about planning a Vegas party

We recommend booking at least 6–8 weeks in advance for weekend trips. Popular clubs, helicopter tours, and pool parties sell out fast — especially on holiday weekends and during peak season (March–May, September–November).

The Las Vegas Strip (between Mandalay Bay and the Wynn) is the best area for party groups. You'll be walking distance from top clubs, restaurants, and entertainment. Midstrip near the Bellagio and MGM Grand is especially central.

Most Vegas bachelor party weekends cost $500–$1,500 per person depending on hotel choice, nightclub spend, and activities. Budget $150–$300/night for hotel, $100–$200 for nightclub entry and table minimums, and $100–$250 for experiences like helicopter tours.

Yes — most top Vegas clubs enforce a strict dress code. Men: no athletic wear, no shorts, collared shirts or button-downs preferred. Women generally have more flexibility. Upscale attire always gets you in faster and may waive cover charges.

Top group experiences include the Las Vegas Strip Helicopter Night Flight, the Fremont Street History Walking Tour, the Sphere immersive experience, exotic car racing, and pool party day clubs like Marquee Dayclub and Wet Republic.
